Offroad routes stay intentionally outside the SPA.

These pages are here to contrast the SPA experience with ordinary full page navigation. They share the same visual shell, but they do not load the standalone bootstrap.

Why keep them around

They make it easier to prove the difference between partial swaps and full reloads without leaving the example set.
