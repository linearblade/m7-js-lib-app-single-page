Why midpoint routes matter

Sequence testing

Mid-stack routes tell us more than edge routes do. If this page restores correctly after a deeper route, then the popstate mapping is holding onto the right URL and the right handler key.

Recommended checks
  • Move forward to Page 3 and confirm the ticker adds another chip.
  • Use Back once and confirm you land here, not on a duplicate or stale layout.
  • Use Back again and make sure the Home scene restores cleanly.
Stack position

This route is intentionally a good “middle state” for testing how history unfolds when the browser owns the navigation.

Visual difference

The tint and wording here differ enough from the neighboring pages that a wrong restore tends to stand out immediately.

Low ceremony

No framework chrome is hiding the behavior. It is just the standalone bundle, the DOM, and the history stack.
