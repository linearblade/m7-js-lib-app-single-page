Why this page matters

If the history envelope or popstate registration is slightly wrong, midpoint routes often reveal it first. They are the ones most likely to show a mismatch between the URL, the title, and the restored body content.

What to notice
  • The ticker should keep earlier route notes visible as newer ones are appended.
  • The masthead should remain stable while only this content area changes.
  • Back should land here exactly, not merely “close enough.”
