Edge route

Good place to shake things loose

When you begin the back sequence here, the restore path has nowhere further to go forward. That makes it easier to spot accidental push behavior or a handler that replays the wrong URL.

What should happen
  • The ticker should note the restore events without clearing earlier messages.
  • The content should step through Page 2 and Page 1 on the way back.
  • The final return to Home should bring the original intro cards back intact.
History honesty
